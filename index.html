About

I am an AI researcher working on medical imaging, focused on building lightweight and reliable deep learning systems for real-world deployment. My work integrates explainable AI (XAI) and uncertainty quantification (UQ) to improve trust and robustness in clinical systems.

Research Focus

  • Lightweight deep learning models for healthcare
  • Multi-scale feature extraction for medical imaging
  • Explainable AI (XAI) for interpretability
  • Uncertainty quantification for reliability
  • Attention and transformer-based architectures
  • Hybrid AI (deep learning + classical ML)
  • Self-supervised learning
  • End-to-end ML pipelines
  • Model validation and deployment readiness
