About

I am an AI researcher working on medical imaging, with a focus on building lightweight and reliable deep learning systems for real-world deployment. My work integrates explainable AI and uncertainty quantification to improve trust and robustness in clinical systems. I am interested in bridging research and industry by designing efficient architectures and scalable AI pipelines.

Research Focus Lightweight and efficient deep learning models for healthcare applications (Explainability + uncertainty focus) Multi-scale feature extraction for complex medical imaging data Development of computer vision systems for detection, classification, and segmentation Integration of Explainable AI (XAI) for improved interpretability and clinical trust Uncertainty quantification for reliable decision-making in safety-critical systems Attention-based and transformer architectures for enhanced feature learning Hybrid AI approaches combining deep learning with classical machine learning methods Self-supervised and representation learning for data-efficient training End-to-end machine learning pipeline development (data processing to deployment) Model validation, calibration, and performance analysis for real-world applications
